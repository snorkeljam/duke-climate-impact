Office of Climate & Sustainability

Sustainable Campus Operations

Duke achieved carbon neutrality in 2024 — the first large research university on the East Coast to do so. Three off-campus solar arrays will supply 50% of campus electricity by mid-2025, with a 2050 net-zero goal now being set.
